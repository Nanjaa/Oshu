Welcome to Oshu: A Fulfillment Odyssey!

To play:

  • Complete your bucket list to the right. Items will be crossed out as they are completed.
  • Use your mouse - You won't need your keyboard in this game.
  • You will earn money over time, which is displayed in your inventory to the right
  • Traveling from planet to planet will cost you time - be careful!
