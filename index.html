A Fulfillment Odyssey

To Play:

  • Traverse the galaxy and complete your Bucket List. Items will be crossed out as they are completed.
  • Use your mouse - You won't need your keyboard in this game.
  • Don't press the back arrow, refresh, or use your backspace key - You'll lose your game!
  • You will earn money over time, which is displayed in your inventory.
  • Traveling from planet to planet will cost you time - be careful!
